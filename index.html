Season Format

  • 7-week regular season
  • 4 groups of 4 teams
  • Each team plays 3 group matches + 3 non-group matches
  • All-Star Week & Roster Lock after Week 4
  • Post-draft: average team MMR assigned; the highest-MMR group is the “Group of Death”

Playoffs

  • 12-team (potentially modified) double-elimination bracket
  • #1 seeds: qualify + first-round bye
  • #2 & #3 seeds: qualify
  • #4 seeds: miss playoffs

About the League

Project V RL is a fan-created virtual esports league where custom teams compete in a structured season. Inspired by real-world football, we feature full rosters, schedules, standings, and commentary.

Our Goals

  • Provide a competitive platform for fantasy-based clubs
  • Track statistics, performance, and team development
  • Host streams and community tournaments
